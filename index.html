About Us

It was Valentine's Day, 2018 when Josh and Gabby had their first conversation on Tinder. After discovering a mutual interest in Twin Peaks and actor Jemaine Clement, they engaged in some fun back and forth conversation and shortly afterward had their first date on the 17th. Officially becoming a couple happened shortly thereafter on the 27th. Since then, many adventures have been had (from Portland Maine to Seattle Washington), as well as the reception addition of an adorable shiba inu.

One year and three months later (May 20th 2019), they became engaged at Niagara Falls. And now, after over 2 years together, the time is right to start their lives together!
